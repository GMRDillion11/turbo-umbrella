About Our University

Ohwowhiahiyeren University, located in Nigeria, is a prestigious institution dedicated to academic excellence, innovation, and leadership. Established to provide top-tier education in various disciplines, the university is committed to shaping future leaders with strong ethical and professional values.

Our Mission

To deliver quality education that meets global standards, foster research and innovation, and produce graduates who will make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a world-class university recognized for excellence in teaching, learning, research, and community service.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Excellence
  • Innovation
  • Inclusiveness
  • Discipline
